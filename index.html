NAVIGATE WITH

ARROW


ARROW aims to enhance the user’s navigation experience inside the university by providing accurate maps, real-time directions, and contextual information about the real-world environment. In this application, the navigation system will only be used inside the University of the East, Manila’s premises.





FEATURES


Introducing the ultimate augmented reality map app designed exclusively for our university. Experience voice navigation, real-time directions, dual view, and shortest path functionality, all in one seamless package. Effortlessly explore your campus with voice instructions, ensuring you never miss a turn. Switch between a traditional map view and an augmented reality overlay for a comprehensive understanding of your surroundings. Embrace the future of campus exploration with this groundbreaking augmented reality map app.
